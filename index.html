Executive Summary

This proposal outlines two comprehensive solutions to transform your current booking system from a rigid, hardcoded structure into a dynamic, user-friendly platform that empowers your team to manage content independently while providing an enhanced customer experience.

Both options address the critical issue of hardcoded pricing and content management, implementing a robust CMS infrastructure that eliminates the need for developer intervention for routine updates. Additionally, both solutions incorporate your requested booking flow enhancement with the new 'Review & Extras' step.

🎯 Key Benefits

  • Complete autonomy over pricing and content updates
  • Enhanced booking flow with transparent pricing breakdown

Technical Implementation Details

CMS Architecture

The CMS implementation will utilise Webflow's native CMS capabilities with custom collections for:

  • Products/Services: Including pricing, descriptions, images, and availability
  • Rooms: With dynamic pricing, capacity, amenities, and gallery
  • Bookings: Storing customer data, selections, and transaction details
  • Coupons: Managing discount codes, validity periods, and usage limits

Coupon System Architecture

While Webflow doesn't natively support complex discount logic, we'll implement a custom solution:

  • CMS collection for coupon management with fields for code, discount type, amount, and validity
  • JavaScript-based validation at checkout to apply discounts
  • Integration with payment processor to ensure accurate pricing
  • Optional: External microservice for advanced coupon logic if needed
